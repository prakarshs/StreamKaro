Our innovative live streaming application, powered by Kafka and built on the robust Spring Boot framework, offers a seamless and engaging experience for both content creators and viewers. With our platform, content creators can effortlessly upload their videos to AWS S3, leveraging its scalability and reliability for storage. Using Kafka, we enable real-time streaming capabilities, allowing content creators to start their broadcasts instantly.

To ensure security and authentication, we've integrated Okta sign-in, providing a trusted and user-friendly way for streamers to access their accounts. Additionally, we've implemented service discovery to enhance scalability and a configuration server to streamline configuration management. What sets our app apart is its user-friendly approach—no sign-up is required for viewers, making it easy for anyone to enjoy the live streams. Whether you're a budding streamer or an avid viewer, our platform offers a dynamic and accessible way to connect with audiences and share captivating content
